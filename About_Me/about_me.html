HOME ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Vishal and welcome to my learning journal.

I’m currently pursuing Computer Science and Engineering, with a passion for Flutter, Firebase, and building full-stack mobile and web applications. I'm constantly experimenting with new technologies, especially around AI and automation.

After learning the fundamentals, I started building real-world apps — from note-taking tools to smart assistants and 5G test utilities. This journal is a record of my progress, reflections, and projects.

How I stay committed to learning

I believe in learning by building. Every time I learn a new concept in Flutter, Firebase, or AI, I implement it in small projects. I also challenge myself with complex ideas like integrating ML models into mobile apps or syncing IDEs across PCs.

This journal helps me stay focused, reflect on my growth, and hopefully inspire others on a similar journey.

How I got started

I began by creating simple apps using Dart and Flutter. Over time, I integrated Firebase for real-time backend support, explored APIs, and even trained and deployed TFLite models for real-time predictions. Documenting each step helped reinforce my learning.

Now, I’m exploring automation, building AI-powered tools, and even experimenting with content generation using platforms like Sora and Stable Video Diffusion.
